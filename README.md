# Technical Strategy and Innovation Projects

Collection of interdisciplinary projects focused on technical strategy, innovation management, healthcare commercialization, AI consulting, global health systems, and humanitarian engineering.

These projects were completed as part of interdisciplinary coursework and collaborative engineering, healthcare, and innovation-focused initiatives within biomedical engineering and technical strategy programs. They combine analytical research, stakeholder analysis, commercialization strategy, systems thinking, and technical communication across healthcare, biomedical engineering, and emerging technologies.


## Repository Structure:

Strategic business and innovation analysis exploring organizational operations, competitive positioning, and business decision-making frameworks within a global consumer company.

**Topics covered:**

- Innovation management
- Market positioning
- Corporate operations
